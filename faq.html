    • 3-Dec-2024 / Takosa Food


    1. What's the entire process of the transaction after order placement?

    Order Confirmed → Prepayment Made → Packaging Contents Confirmed → Production Finished → Containers Loaded → Documents COnfirmed/Preview → Balance Payment Made → Original Docs Sent → Shipment Arrived → Customs Clearance → Goods Received/Confirmed → After-Sales Support.


    2. What's the MOQ?

    It depends on the product and packaging form. Mixed container loading is available. Just tell your plans to our sales representatives. We will try the best to meet your actual needs and come up with a solution.


    3. Custom Packaging?

    Both private labeling and custom inner/outer packaging are well supported. Please notice that it might cost extra expenses to order color-printing bags. The bag-makers generally would ask template-making charge (only for the first printing) and a printing MOQ of 20000pcs (for each time). Of course, we will strive to minimize the related cost for customers.


    4. What's the lead time?

    In normal situations, we could finish 1x40ft in 15~35days (included order queuing and packaging making). However we recommend you to order in advance as ocean shipping is becoming more and more of a problem in these couple of years.
